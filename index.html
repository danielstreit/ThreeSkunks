You're trapped in a hallway with three doors. Behind each door is an exit blocked by a skunk! One of the skunks has been descented. Choose your door.

You've choosen door . Basking in their benevolence, the gamemakers say:

Out of the goodness of our hearts, we will help you avoid a stinky situation. From the two doors you have not selected, we will remove one that definately has a stinky skunk behind it. The remaining door may also have a stinky skunk, or it may have the descented skunk. Do you want to keep your first choice or switch doors?
-the gamemakers

Congratulations!

You found the cute and cuddly skunk with no stink.

The gamemakers are proud of you:

You played the best strategy and won! You are worthy of your lack of stench.
-the gamemakers

The gamemakers are upset with your victory:

You played a poor strategy and won. How dissappointing! You should reek of skunk!
-the gamemakers

Ewwwww!

You got sprayed by a stinky skunk!

The gamemakers sympathize:

We hate to see good strategy lose, but sometimes that's the way it goes. We have prepared a tomato juice bath to help remove your stench.
-the gamemakers

The gamemakers are delighted:

Haha YES, you played a poor strategy and got sprayed by a skunk. It's moments like these we make games for.
-the gamemakers
